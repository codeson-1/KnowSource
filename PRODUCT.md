# Product

## Register

product

## Users

KnowSource is used by an interviewer, project reviewer, or internal operator who needs to verify an enterprise RAG workflow quickly: create a knowledge base, upload and publish documents, ask grounded questions, inspect citations, and review trace/evaluation evidence.

## Product Purpose

KnowSource demonstrates a complete RAG application rather than a chat wrapper. Success means a user can understand the document ingestion, publish/index, Q&A, citation, QaTrace, member, admin, and evaluation flows from the console without reading backend internals.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, technical. The interface should feel like an operations console for trustworthy knowledge retrieval: composed, legible, and confident under demo pressure.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page hero layouts, oversized decorative cards, novelty controls, generic gradient SaaS visuals, and chat-only interfaces that hide ingestion/index state. The product should not look like a toy chatbot or a plain CRUD scaffold.

## Design Principles

1. Make state visible: document, ingest, index, retrieval, and trace states should be easy to scan at a glance.
2. Keep the demo path direct: login, choose a knowledge base, upload, publish, ask, cite, and inspect trace should all feel close at hand.
3. Earn trust with evidence: citations, metrics, and operational status should be visually connected to the actions that produced them.
4. Use restrained product polish: familiar controls, clear hierarchy, and consistent spacing matter more than visual spectacle.
5. Preserve technical density: this is a tool for repeated inspection, so tables, filters, and panels can be compact as long as they remain readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support keyboard focus visibility, reduced-motion preferences, readable Chinese and English mixed labels, and color-independent status indicators through labels plus tone.
